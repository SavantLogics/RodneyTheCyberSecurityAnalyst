About Me

I have graduated from California State University, Long Beach (CSULB). In my last year, I was placed on the Dean's Honor List and the President's Honor Roll. Afterwards, I signed up for the military and finished my career in the Navy. During that time, I did two deployments and I received three Navy And Marine Corps Achievement Medals. After finishing my contract, I returned to school to learn applicable skills regarding cybersecurity. I graduated from Long Beach City College with a 4.0 along with receiving two A.S. degrees in Computer Networking / Computer Security and Web Development. I became a cyber security analyst for Lynwood Unified School District in Lynwood, California.

I went back to school to get my graduate degree. I have now graduated from University of North Texas with a Master's degree in Advanced Data Analytics. I am starting the next chapter of my life working for DHL Supply Chain Company.

I possess a strong understanding of data analysis techniques and a solid foundation in cybersecurity concepts. I am confident in applying this knowledge to real-world scenarios and contributing to my organization's success. My academic background has equipped me with relevant skills, including data processing, statistical analysis, and programming languages like Python, R, and SQL. In addition, my experience collaborating with diverse teams and effectively communicating technical information makes me an asset to any workplace.
