My Journey

I graduated from California State University, Long Beach (CSULB) with honors, earning placement on both the Dean's Honor List and President's Honor Roll. Following my passion for service, I joined the U.S. Navy where I served as a SONAR technician, completing two deployments and receiving three Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medals.

After my military service, I pursued cybersecurity education at Long Beach City College, graduating with a perfect 4.0 GPA and earning dual A.S. degrees in Computer Networking/Computer Security and Web Development. I then worked as a cybersecurity analyst for Lynwood Unified School District.

Recently, I completed my Master's degree in Advanced Data Analytics from the University of North Texas (Go Mean Green!) and have joined DHL Supply Chain Company, ready to tackle new challenges in data analysis and business intelligence.
